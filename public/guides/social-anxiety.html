Social Anxiety Self-Help Manual

When facing social anxiety, you can try:

  1. Set small goals and gradually adapt to social situations.
  2. Record positive experiences after each social interaction.
  3. Practice self-affirmation and reduce self-criticism.
  4. Seek professional psychological help when needed.
