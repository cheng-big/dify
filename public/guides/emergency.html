Emotional First Aid Guide

If you feel overwhelmed or helpless, try the following five steps to self-regulate:

  1. Take deep breaths to stabilize your emotions.
  2. Acknowledge your feelings—don't suppress them.
  3. Write down your thoughts and feelings at this moment.
  4. Talk to someone you trust or seek professional help.
  5. Give yourself time and allow your emotions to settle gradually.
