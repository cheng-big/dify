Emotional First Aid Guide

If you feel overwhelmed or helpless, try the following five steps for emotional first aid. Your AI mental health companion is here to provide emotional support, AI chat, and mood tracking whenever you need it.

  1. Take deep breaths to stabilize your emotions.
  2. Acknowledge your feelings—don’t suppress them.
  3. Write down your thoughts and feelings at this moment.
  4. Talk to someone you trust, use AI chat, or seek professional help.
  5. Give yourself time and allow your emotions to settle gradually.
